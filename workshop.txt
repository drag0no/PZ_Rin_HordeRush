description=Nowhere is safe forever.

description=[b]"Horde Rush"[/b] is a survival-focused mod designed to keep you on your toes and test your base defenses when you least expect it... Are you prepared to hold the line?
description=
description=
description=[h2] HOW IT WORKS [/h2]
description=
description=The event cycles through three phases:
description=
description=* [b]The 'Cooldown' Phase[/b] - The time between events. Gameplay is normal and zombies will behave as usual.
description=
description=* [b]The 'Calm' Phase[/b] - The period in which mass zombie migration occurs. Towns and cities will seem deserted and eerily quiet. This gives players the opportunity to scavenge and build defenses before the inevitable horde arrives.
description=
description=* [b]The 'Storm' Phase[/b] - Zombies return in massive hordes and persistently hunt down the player(s). Zombies are, by default, always aware of the player's position, which can be changed in the sandbox menu.
description=
description=
description=[h2] NOTES [/h2]
description=
description=* [b]No Artificial Spawns:[/b] This mod does not spawn new zombies out of thin air. It only attracts the undead that are already roaming nearby in your world.
description=
description=* [b]Sandbox Scaling:[/b] Because it uses the existing local population, the difficulty and size of these waves are directly tied to your game's default ZombieConfig in the Sandbox Variables. Play with high population multipliers, and you'll face massive, overwhelming hordes. Play on lower populations, and the rushes will be more manageable.
