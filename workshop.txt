description=[h2] HOW IT WORKS [/h2]
description=
description=- [b]The Swarm:[/b] At periodic intervals, all existing zombies within a surrounding radius will be drawn directly to your location.
description=- [b]No Artificial Spawns:[/b] This mod does not spawn new zombies out of thin air. It only attracts the undead that are already roaming nearby in your world.
description=- [b]Sandbox Scaling:[/b] Because it uses the existing local population, the difficulty and size of these waves are directly tied to your game's default ZombieConfig in the Sandbox Variables. Play with high population multipliers, and you'll face massive, overwhelming hordes. Play on lower populations, and the rushes will be more manageable.
description=- [b]Defend or Die:[/b] Whether you're farming, sleeping, or organizing your loot, you will suddenly find yourself in a desperate standoff. Fortify your safe-houses, stockpile ammunition, and never let your guard down.
